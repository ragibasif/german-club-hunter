About Us

Our Mission

We will bring more German Culture to Hunter College. We will immerse ourselves into German film, art, music, and food in a casual setting. Whether you're a student/faculty of The German Department or if you are just curious about the German language and culture, all are welcome!

Why Join?

  • Many opportunities to make new friends and immerse yourself in another culture.
  • Give yourself a chance to practice your German, even if you are not fluent.
  • There is also going to be food at most of the events, so it gives everyone a chance to eat and chat.
  • Get help with applying for Study Abroad.

The Team

Events

You can expect events such as:

  • Movie screenings (with English subtitles)
  • Excursions to museums, Goethe Institut, Frederic Morton Library and German restaurants.
  • Creative workshops for exploring German art, music, and literature (with English translation)
  • Open Mic events for poetry or karaoke
  • Kaffeeklatsch (get together and drink coffee/tea)

Membership

Membership requirements: Have a genuine interest in the German language and culture.
